Early Life

Allu Arjun was born on April 8, 1983, in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, to Allu Aravind, a film producer, and Nirmala. He grew up in Hyderabad, Telangana, and completed his schooling at St. Patrick's High School in Hyderabad. From a young age, Allu Arjun was interested in dance and was trained in various dance forms like Bharatanatyam, Kuchipudi, and Western dance styles. He used to participate in various dance competitions and won several awards for his performances.

Career

Allu Arjun started his acting career in 2003 with the film Gangotri. He gained popularity with the films Arya and Bunny, which were released in 2004 and 2005 respectively. He has since acted in numerous Telugu and Tamil films and has won several awards for his performances.

Awards

  • Nandi Award for Best Actor for his performance in Parugu
  • Filmfare Award for Best Actor - Telugu for his performance in Arya 2
  • South Indian International Movie Award for Best Actor for his performance in Race Gurram
  • IIFA Award for Best Performance in a Leading Role - Male for his performance in Rudhramadevi

Personal Life

Allu Arjun married Sneha Reddy in 2011 and they have two children, a son named Ayaan and a daughter named Arha. He is known for his philanthropic work and has been involved in various charitable causes.

Philanthropy

Allu Arjun has been involved in various philanthropic activities. He has supported and contributed to initiatives related to education, health, and poverty alleviation. In 2017, he adopted a village called Erra Pallem in Telangana and contributed towards its development. He has also been a part of campaigns to raise awareness about social issues such as child labor and sanitation.
